Special Track: Reinforcement Learning for Combinatorial Optimizations

The Special Track: Reinforcement Learning for Combinatorial Optimizations invites researchers, academics, and industry professionals to submit original papers on Reinforcement Learning (RL) technologies for Combinatorial Optimizations (CO). CO problems in operations research (OR) areas are generally NP-Hard. This Special Track aims to showcase cutting-edge research, foster collaboration, and address challenges in CO problems using RL, especially in the distribution-wise scenario.

The Special Track will explore the future of RL-driven operations research and contribute to innovative solutions for a connected world with special interest in but not limited to:

  • RL & Machine Learning especially in the distribution-wise scenario: Deep Learning, and AI for CO/OR.
  • Typical CO in OR: graph maxcut problem, knapsack problem, number partitioning problem, etc.
  • Transportation: vehicle routing problem (VRP), vehicle scheduling problem, path planning problem in transportation, ridesharing, etc.
  • Communication Systems: RL-enabled 5G/6G, cognitive radio, SDN.
  • Edge Computing & IoT: Edge intelligence, IoT security, real-time RL applications for internet of vehicles (IoV), IoT.
  • Emerging Trends: Quantum AI, green AI, AI ethics, future RL research for OR or science.
