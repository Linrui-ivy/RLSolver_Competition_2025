Call for Papers

The Special Track on Reinforcement Learning for Combinatorial Optimizations invites researchers, academics, and industry professionals to submit original papers on RL technologies for CO problems, which are generally NP-Hard in operations research (OR).

Topics of Interest

  • RL & Machine Learning in the distribution-wise scenario
  • Typical CO: graph maxcut, knapsack, number partitioning
  • Transportation: VRP, scheduling, path planning, ridesharing
  • Communication: RL-enabled 5G/6G, cognitive radio, SDN
  • Edge Computing & IoT: edge intelligence, IoT security, RL for IoV
  • Emerging: quantum AI, green AI, AI ethics, future RL in science

Submission Guidelines

Please submit your paper via the official portal.
